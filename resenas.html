Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book?
We recommend booking at least 8 weeks before your travel dates so we can secure the best hotel and flight availability.
Can I customize a package?
Yes. Every package is a starting point — tell us your priorities on the contact page and we'll adjust the itinerary.
Is airfare between cities included?
Yes, all three packages include national flights between Bogotá, Medellín, and Cartagena.
Do I need a visa to enter Colombia?
Requirements vary by nationality — check your country's requirements before booking.
