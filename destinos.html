Top Colombia Destinations

Three cities, three completely different sides of Colombia — pick one or visit all three on a single trip.

Bogotá

Explore museums, culture, and incredible gastronomy in Colombia's capital city, cradled by the Andes.

  • Gold Museum & historic La Candelaria
  • Monserrate mountain viewpoint
  • World-class restaurant scene

Medellín

Enjoy beautiful spring-like weather, innovation, vibrant nightlife, and views over green mountains.

  • Metrocable rides over the city
  • Comuna 13 street art tours
  • Rooftop bars in El Poblado
Official Medellín travel guide ↗

Cartagena

Relax on Caribbean beaches and wander the colorful streets of the historic walled city.

  • Ciudad Amurallada walled old town
  • Rosario Islands day trips
  • Sunset views from the city walls
